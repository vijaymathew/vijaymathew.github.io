<h1>The Well-Set Typographer</h1>
<h2 style="font-weight:400; font-size:1.4rem; margin-top:0;">Typography, Typesetting, and Document Production from the Command Line</h2>
<p>This book teaches you to produce documents that are not merely formatted but <em>typeset</em> — built from systems that enforce consistency, enable reproducibility, and produce output of a quality that word processors cannot match. Every tool, technique, and principle is driven from the command line.</p>
